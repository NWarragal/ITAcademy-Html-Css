Speed is our life

We present you the official racing championship - Formula 1, where the top 20 pilots of the world compete with each other. Passions, raging blood, adrenaline - it's all a Formula 1.

What is Formula 1?

Formula 1 is the pinnacle of motor racing, where the best pilots and the best automakers of the world perform. Specifically for racing are built specific cars, that are the fastest on the planet.

High tecnologies

Each machine is a work of art created with the participation of a large number of engineers, mechanics and is the result of the coordinated work of a huge system of companies.

One year - one car

Dynamically changing regulations do not allow manufacturers to bring in the new year the old car, so what's there in the year, the cars are being finalized during the whole season!

Big money

Big prizes are for the strongest, and the payment is appropriate here, but manufacturers report a lot from their own pockets - the average budget is estimated at about $ 200 million!

Races all around the world

21 racing stages. 21 host countries. Virtually all continents. and this is not the limit - the organizers plan to expand the number of races to 25! Maybe Formula 1 will come to you?
